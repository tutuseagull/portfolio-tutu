Launch a New Investment Product

Overview

Design Duration: 3.5 months (2018)

Client Industry

Proptech, Fintech

User

Accredited Investors

Role

Product designer (* Collaborated with a team in user research and another designer in the web design process)

Services

User research, responsive web design, UX testing, marketing assets & document design

Purpose

Design and launch a new real estate investment service, and build a website to support that.

Approach

  • Study & Learn about the investment behaviors of our potential user
  • Explain a relatively new and complex investment model to the users visually and verbally
  • Provide Visual and Verbal Explanation towards new & complex investment model for users
  • Validate business models
  • Build trust between the investors and the platform through a responsive website
  • Work with existing brand DNA and create a new style guide for the targeted visitors
